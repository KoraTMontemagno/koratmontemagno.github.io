# Hej there! Some info here ;)
This very not well done website was born with a purpouse: to orient researchers that want to transition from another field to experiment with *human sexuality*.

Often neglected by neuroscience, sexual pleasure is researched by few brave people that may want to share informal connections, know-how, experiences. For example: how do you become a sex neuroscientist? 

I myself am not one: my PhD is on pain perception and electrophysiological brain signals. Will I be able to transition from pain to pleasure? Well, only time will tell...


But this is not just a "How to" manual - because why not? It is a messy collection of thoughts of a person that hasn't find their path in life and wants more than what their experiences in Academia have offered them so far. Do you relate to this? ;)
